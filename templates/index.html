HSV histogram, HOG, LBP texture, body proportions, symmetry - 50 interpretable dims.

04 /

About

One model, three outputs, plus an explanation per call.

CarSpec AI reads a vehicle like a spec sheet. A MobileNetV2 backbone handles the deep features; 50 handcrafted features (HSV histogram, HOG, LBP, symmetry, body proportions) handle the interpretable side. One forward pass returns type, doors, seats, and a sentence explaining each.
